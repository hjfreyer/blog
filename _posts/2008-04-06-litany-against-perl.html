--- layout: post title: Litany Against Perl date: '2008-04-06T17:48:00.001-04:00' author: Hunter Freyer tags: modified_time: '2008-04-06T17:48:51.322-04:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-203463942347409368.post-5123401265694435533 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.hjfreyer.com/2008/04/litany-against-perl.html --- I must not use Perl.
Perl is the mind-killer.
Perl is the little-death that brings total obliteration.
I will face my script.
I will permit it to run over me and through me.
And when it has finished I will turn on the debugger to trace its stack.
Where the Perl has gone there will be nothing.
Only I will remain.

Blatantly stolen from Frank Herbert's Litany Against Fear.
