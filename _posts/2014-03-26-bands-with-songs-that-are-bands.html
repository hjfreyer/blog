For instance, Big Audio Dynamite has a song called "Rush", and Rush is a band. What if Rush has a song that's another band? How far could we go with this? Wouldn't it be crazy if we had a chain like, 10 or 15 long?

So I decided to find out. I downloaded the Musicbrainz database, wrote a little Go code, and I was shocked to find, almost immediately, a path of length 7,582. And that only includes songs written in Latin script; with Cyrillic and Japanese I was seeing paths longer than 9,000!

Finding the absolute longest path is NP-complete (aka, really hard), so to find this path I used a simple "random restarts" heuristic. There could easily be longer ones out there, but for now I'm satisfied.
